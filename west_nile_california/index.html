West Nile Virus in California 2006-2015

New cases of West Nile virus were reported weekly in California. The map on the left illustrates the yearly outbreak, by county. The map is normalized to show the number of cases per 100,000 people. The bar charts on the right show the raw number of cases reported each week. We see that the outbreaks occur in the summer, and follow a bell-curve. Select sections on the bottom chart to see them in detail on the top.
